    Conclusions

    Through this analysis, we hope that users have found insights into patterns in Chicago criminal activity, and perhaps how to better combat that criminal activity. We hope that the visualizations and insights from them help to make decisions on how to best allocate scarce city resources, when and where to bolster police presence within the city, which types of crimes to focus on combatting, and how to best protect the citizens of Chicago overall! While this analysis is not a definitive list of measures to be taken, it can certainly lead to great insights and we hope that it can be of some use to those Chicago city officials in charge of keeping the city and its people safe.
